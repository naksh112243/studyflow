## 🎯 Ek feature jo mujhe lagta hai app ko memorable bana dega

Ye feature maine kisi existing study timetable app me exactly is form me nahi dekha:

**Adaptive Day Score**

Subah app silently calculate kare:

* Kitna late uthe?
* Kitne sessions bache?
* Kya realistically complete ho sakta hai?

Aur top par sirf ek line:

🟢 **"Today's plan is achievable."**

ya

🟡 **"Today's plan has been adjusted after your late start."**

Ya agar bahut delay ho gaya:

🔴 **"Today's plan has been simplified to keep you consistent."**

Ye user ko guilt nahi deta. Ye bas reality ke hisaab se din ko adapt karta hai. Aur mujhe lagta hai, government exam aspirants ke liye ye emotional aur practical dono level par bahut valuable feature hoga.
