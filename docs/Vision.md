### Vision

> Government exam aspirants ke liye duniya ka sabse simple aur distraction-free adaptive study timetable.

### Principles

- Less than 4 daily user actions.
    
- Zero unnecessary notifications.
    
- Offline-first.
    
- No feature bloat.
    
- One-screen-first design.
    
- Fast enough ki app 1 second ke andar khul jaye.
    
- Har feature ka answer hona chahiye: "Kya isse user ka effort kam hua?"

# 💎 Research se nikle 5 Product Principles

1. **One tap should solve today's problem.**
2. **Missed sessions should never destroy the entire day.**
3. **The app adapts to the student, not the other way around.**
4. **Every extra input is a bug unless it provides clear value.**
5. **Open app → Know what to study now → Close app.**
