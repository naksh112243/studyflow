# 🎯 Research Goal

Ek sawal ka answer:

> **Government exam aspirants ke liye existing apps kya miss kar rahi hain, aur hum kya better bana sakte hain?**

---

# 👤 Target User (Primary Persona)

## Primary User

* Age: 18 to 35
* Preparing for: RRB, SSC, UPSC, Banking, State PSC, Railway, Police
* Daily routine:

  * Wake up
  * Study
  * Eat
  * Study
  * Sleep
* Wants:

  * Consistency
  * Simple routine
  * Zero planning headache

**Important observation:**

Ye log productivity expert nahi hote.

Ye bas roz padhna chahte hain.

# 😤 Current Problems

Research aur Reddit se kuch patterns baar baar dikhe.

### Problem 1

Planning me hi 15 minute chale jaate hain.

---

### Problem 2

Ek session miss hua.

Pura timetable toot gaya.

Todait jaise apps isi pain point ko address karte hain by automatically rebalancing remaining work. ([Todait][1])

---

### Problem 3

Bahut saari apps me features itne zyada hain ki users overwhelmed feel karte hain. Reviews aur community discussions me repeatedly "too organized", "too complicated" aur feature overload ki complaints milti hain. ([App Store][2])

---

### Problem 4

Late uth gaye.

Timetable kharab.

App help nahi karti.

---

### Problem 5

Study planner apps mostly schools/universities ke liye bani hain.

Government exam aspirants ka workflow alag hota hai.

# 💎 Research se nikle 5 Product Principles

1. **One tap should solve today's problem.**
2. **Missed sessions should never destroy the entire day.**
3. **The app adapts to the student, not the other way around.**
4. **Every extra input is a bug unless it provides clear value.**
5. **Open app → Know what to study now → Close app.**

---

# 🚨 Mujhe ek aur bahut bada gap mila

Almost **saari apps assignment based hain**, na ki **routine based**.

Example:

```
Finish Chapter 5

Submit Homework

Read Biology

Complete Assignment
```

Lekin government exam aspirant aise nahi padhta.

Uska routine hota hai:

```
6:00 Wake

7:00 Math

9:00 Physics

11:00 Reasoning

2:00 Mock Test

8:00 Revision
```

Ye **daily routine** hai, task list nahi.

🎯 **Mujhe lagta hai ye hi app ka biggest differentiator hoga.**

Hum "study planner" nahi bana rahe.

Hum **"Daily Study Routine Operating System"** bana rahe hain.

Aur honestly, ye positioning mujhe existing competitors se kaafi alag aur focused lagti hai.
